Restaurant Services

Reliable Plumbing Solutions for South Jersey Restaurants

Running a restaurant means you can’t afford plumbing problems. From clogged drains to broken grease traps, even a small issue can disrupt your kitchen and affect your customers. That’s where Bukowski Plumbing comes in.

For over 40 years, our family-owned and operated company has provided professional restaurant plumbing services across South Jersey, including Cherry Hill, Moorestown, Mt. Laurel, Pennsauken, Deptford, Collingswood, Maple Shade, and Haddonfield. We understand the fast-paced demands of the food service industry — and we respond quickly to keep your kitchen running safely and efficiently.

Full-Service Restaurant Plumbing Experts

Our licensed plumbers specialize in commercial-grade plumbing systems built for restaurants, bars, cafés, and food service facilities. Whether you’re dealing with a plumbing emergency or planning a new kitchen installation, Bukowski Plumbing has the experience and equipment to get the job done right.

Our restaurant plumbing services include:

  • Emergency plumbing repairs (24/7 service available)
  • Grease trap installation, cleaning, and maintenance
  • Commercial drain cleaning and hydro jetting
  • Sink, faucet, and dishwasher hookups
  • Restroom and fixture installation
  • Water heater and hot water system maintenance
  • Backflow prevention testing and certification
  • Sewer line inspection and repair

Supporting South Jersey’s Food Service Industry

We’re proud to serve the local restaurants, cafés, and catering businesses that make South Jersey special. Whether you manage a family diner in Haddonfield, a busy café in Collingswood, or a fine dining restaurant in Moorestown, you can depend on Bukowski Plumbing for professional, reliable plumbing support.

Why Restaurants Choose Bukowski Plumbing

As a local, family-owned business, we treat every client like a neighbor. When you call, you’ll talk directly with someone who knows the trade — not an automated system. We provide fast response times, honest pricing, and quality workmanship that keeps your business compliant and operational.

With Bukowski Plumbing, you get more than just a plumber — you get a trusted partner who understands the unique plumbing needs of South Jersey’s restaurant industry.
