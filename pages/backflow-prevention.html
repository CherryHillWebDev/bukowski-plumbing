Why Backflow Prevention Matters

Backflow occurs when contaminated water reverses flow and enters your clean water supply — often due to pressure changes in the plumbing system. This can introduce harmful substances, bacteria, or chemicals into your potable water. A properly installed backflow preventer is your first line of defense against contamination.

Our team helps homeowners, restaurants, medical facilities, retail spaces, and commercial buildings meet local code requirements and safeguard their plumbing systems with certified backflow testing and device maintenance.
