Who We Are At Bukowski Plumbing Inc.

Family owned, expert craftsmanship, attention to detail, and community are what make us who we are. Join the family today and count on us for your most important investment!

Family Owned, Built on Trust and Craftsmanship

For more than 40 years, Bukowski Plumbing has been South Jersey’s trusted name for residential and commercial plumbing services. Family-owned and operated, we take pride in delivering dependable, high-quality work to homes and businesses across Moorestown, Cherry Hill, Mt. Laurel, Pennsauken, Deptford, Collingswood, Maple Shade, and Haddonfield.

Our licensed plumbers bring decades of hands-on experience to every project — from emergency plumbing repairs and water heater replacements to full bathroom remodels and commercial plumbing maintenance. We understand that plumbing problems can’t wait, which is why our team responds quickly with reliable service that restores comfort, safety, and peace of mind.

Serving Homes, Businesses & Specialized Facilities

Bukowski Plumbing isn’t just a residential plumber — we’re a trusted partner for restaurants, retail spaces, offices, and medical facilities throughout South Jersey. We handle everything from grease trap maintenance and fixture installations to high-demand hot water systems and compliance-focused plumbing for healthcare environments.

Family-Owned, Community-Focused

As a local, family-owned business, we believe good service starts with good communication. When you call Bukowski Plumbing, you’ll speak directly with someone who knows the trade — not a call center. Our customers appreciate the personal touch, honest advice, and commitment to getting the job done right the first time.

We’ve built our reputation the old-fashioned way; through referrals, repeat customers, and consistent results. Whether you’re facing a burst pipe at 2 AM or planning a remodel for your business, you can count on Bukowski Plumbing for fast, professional service backed by decades of South Jersey experience.

About Robert Bukowski - Master Plumber

As a teenager I was always building something, I was always mechanically inclined. In 1977, I started out framing houses for Paprone Construction. In 1980, I decided to attend Camden County Vo-Tech (Sicklerville Campus). I completed two years of plumbing technology. I received my diploma in June 1982.

At the same time, I was working part-time and full-time for a drain cleaning company from 1980 to 1985. I started my own business in June 1985 acquiring local plumbing license in Pennsauken and Camden. I moved the business to Maple Shade in 1993 and acquired my Master Plumbing license in 2002 (license #11801). I expanded the business to Gloucester City in 2007 with an expanded office and warehouse to better serve our customers.

I presently have three trucks and four full time employees. I love what I do for a living and I enjoy helping and making my customers satisfied.
