Real-Estate Services

Plumbing Solutions for Property Managers, Realtors, and Landlords

When you manage multiple properties, you need a plumbing partner who responds quickly, works cleanly, and communicates clearly. Bukowski Plumbing has proudly provided real estate and property management plumbing services across South Jersey for over 40 years — serving Cherry Hill, Moorestown, Mt. Laurel, Pennsauken, Deptford, Collingswood, Maple Shade, and Haddonfield.

Our family-owned company helps realtors, landlords, and property managers keep homes, apartments, and commercial buildings running efficiently and ready for occupancy.

Reliable Plumbing for Every Property Type

We handle everything from pre-sale inspections to emergency repairs — with prompt, professional service and fair, transparent pricing.

Our property-management plumbing services include:

  • Tenant plumbing repair and maintenance
  • Pre-sale and pre-rental plumbing inspections
  • Drain cleaning and sewer maintenance
  • Water heater repair and replacement
  • Fixture installation and upgrades
  • Leak detection and pipe repair
  • Toilet, sink, and shower replacement
  • Main water line and shut-off valve repair
  • Sump pump and flood prevention systems
  • Backflow prevention and testing
  • Emergency plumbing services (24/7)

Serving South Jersey’s Property Professionals

Bukowski Plumbing partners with property managers, homeowners associations, real estate investors, and residential landlords to provide consistent, reliable plumbing service throughout the region. We understand how important quick response times and clear communication are when managing multiple tenants or closing a sale.

Why Property Managers Trust Bukowski Plumbing

We’re more than just plumbers — we’re an extension of your property management team. Our work is timely, clean, and compliant with local regulations. When you call, you’ll reach a real person — someone who knows your property and your priorities.

Whether it’s a rental turnover inspection, a last-minute water heater failure, or a preventive maintenance program, Bukowski Plumbing is your dependable plumbing partner.
