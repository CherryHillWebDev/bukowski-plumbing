Seasonal Plumbing Tips for Maple Shade Homeowners

Maple Shade’s climate brings freezing winters and humid summers. Protect your home with these local tips:

  • Winter: Disconnect outdoor hoses, insulate exposed pipes in basements and crawl spaces, and keep your thermostat above 55°F even when away.
  • Spring: Check for leaky hose bibs, test your sump pump before heavy rains, and schedule a backflow test for irrigation systems.
  • Summer: Avoid garbage disposal clogs by not putting fibrous foods or grease down the drain. Consider a tankless water heater for energy savings.
  • Fall: Have your water heater flushed to remove sediment, and inspect your sewer line for root intrusion.
